## What is this?

**Prism CNC** is a compact, enclosed desktop CNC router designed specifically for **PCB milling and small precision work**.

It’s built mostly from **repurposed Ender 3 components**, custom sheet metal plates, and aluminum extrusion. The goal was to create a **rigid, fully enclosed machine** that’s small enough to live on a desk but powerful enough to mill circuit boards and small parts reliably.

---

## Features

**300mm+ Work Area**
Because tiny CNCs are sad CNCs.

**Fully Enclosed Frame**
Acrylic panels + sheet metal walls keep chips contained and make it look cool.

**Ender 3 Hardware Reuse**
Extrusions and motion components salvaged from broken printers.

**775 Spindle Motor**
Cheap and capable for PCB milling.

**Linear Rails**
For smoother motion and better rigidity than ender 3d printer-style wheels.

**Limit Switches**
So the machine can actually zero itself instead of guessing.

**Electronics Bay**
Dedicated compartment for the controller, motor driver, and power supply.

---

## Structure

The frame is primarily built from both **20x20 and 40x40 aluminum extrusion**, reinforced with **custom sheet metal plates** that tie the structure together.

Side walls use a layered approach:

- 3D printed supports
- Acrylic panels (for diffusion + aesthetics)
- Sheet metal structural plates

This keeps the machine rigid while also making it look less like a pile of metal beams.

---

## Spindle

The machine uses a **775 DC motor** as the spindle. It’s not industrial, but it’s perfect for:

- PCB milling
- engraving
- light cutting operations

And most importantly: it’s cheap and easy to replace.

---

## Electronics

The electronics live in a rear-mounted box that houses the CNC controller and spindle motor driver.
The power supply for the CNC is mounted on the rear side wall.

Keeping everything in one compartment helps keep wiring more manageable and protects the electronics from debris.

---

## Why?

I realized something after getting my first 3D printer:

**Having fabrication tools at home massively speeds up projects.**

Prism CNC is meant to expand that capability by bringing **PCB manufacturing and precision milling** into the same workflow.
